Book Genre Description
Atomic Habits Self help, Personal Development Atomic Habits provides a practical guide for building good habits and breaking bad ones. James Clear introduces the concept of "atomic habits," which are small changes that can lead to significant personal growth over time. The book offers strategies rooted in psychology and neuroscience, helping readers create systems for lasting behavioral change.
Think like a Monk Self help, Spirituality In Think Like a Monk, Jay Shetty shares lessons learned from his time as a monk to help readers find peace, clarity, and purpose in their lives. The book blends ancient wisdom with modern insights, teaching readers how to overcome negativity, reduce stress, and build habits for a fulfilling life.
The Village by the Sea Fiction, Young Adult Literature The Village by the Sea tells the story of a poor family living in a small village in India. The novel focuses on the struggles of two children, Lila and Hari, who face hardship and adversity as they try to provide for their family. Set against the backdrop of industrialization and rural poverty, it is a tale of survival, resilience, and hope.
